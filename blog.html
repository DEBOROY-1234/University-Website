Courses and Academics

Certificates and programs

Jadavpur Univeristy offers a variety of certificate programs that are designed to provide students with specialized skills and knowledge in a particular field of study. Our certificate programs are typically shorter in duration than degree programs, and they are often designed to be completed on a part-time basis, making them a flexible option for students who are working or have other commitments.

Upon completion of a certificate program, students receive a certificate of achievement that can be added to their resume or used to demonstrate their proficiency in a particular area. Our certificate programs are widely recognized by employers and can help students to advance their careers or pursue further education in their chosen field.
