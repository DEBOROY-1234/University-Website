JADAVPUR UNIVERSITY

Jadavpur University is a public state autonomous research university located in Jadavpur, Kolkata, West Bengal, India. Ranked fourth among universities in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF). Achieved 11th rank in the engineering category and 12th rank overall in National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2022
