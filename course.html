PHD Courses

Jadavpur University offers a range of PhD courses across various disciplines to help students further their academic and research pursuits. Our PhD programs are designed to provide students with rigorous training and advanced knowledge in their chosen field, preparing them for successful careers in academia or industry. Some of the popular PhD courses offered at Jadavpur University include Data Science,Artificial Intelligence,Cybersecurity. These courses are taught by experienced faculty members who are experts in their respective fields, and students have the opportunity to work closely with them on research projects.

Our facilities

Central Library

Three-storied building on the campus. Its shelf space and working space
total 36,000 sq. ft. Seven reading rooms, carrels, and an alcove for bound volumes of
journals provide sitting accommodation for 700 readers.There is a separate
library on the Second Campus at Salt Lake.
In addition to our physical collection, our library offers a range of digital resources and services that can be accessed remotely, including e-books, e-journals, and online databases.

PlayGround

Our playground is designed to accommodate various sports and activities, and students can participate in organized games and tournaments or engage in informal play with friends. The space is also suitable for outdoor fitness activities such as yoga and group exercise classes.
We believe that physical activity and outdoor play are essential components of a healthy and balanced lifestyle, and we encourage our students to make use of our playground facilities to stay active and engaged.

Canteens and Restaurants

More than 7 canteens and Restaurants throughout the campus. Discover the best foods from from snacks to desserts at affordable prices and best qualities with the best tastes.
In addition to main meals, our canteen also offers snacks and beverages, including sandwiches, salads, fruit cups, and a range of hot and cold drinks. We understand that students often have busy schedules, and we strive to offer quick and convenient meal options that can be enjoyed on-the-go.
